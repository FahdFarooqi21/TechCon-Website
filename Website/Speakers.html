Meet the Speakers

Bill Gates


Bill Gates, co-founder of Microsoft, stands as a pivotal figure in the evolution of computing. Bills Gates' influence extends beyond the digital realm, as he is a significant philanthropist. Through the Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation, he allocates substantial resources to address critical global challenges such as healthcare, education, and poverty.

Elon Musk


As the founder of SpaceX, Tesla, and co-founder of Neuralink and The Boring Company, Musk has exhibited an unparalleled commitment to innovation. His transformative contributions extend beyond electric vehicles and space exploration, encompassing renewable energy and advancements in artificial intelligence. We're sure you'll like his witty humour too.

Mark Zuckerberg


Mark Zuckerberg, the co-founder and CEO of Facebook, has played a pivotal role in shaping the landscape of social media and digital communication. He transformed a dorm room project into a global platform connecting billions. Zuckerberg's initiatives extend beyond social networking, branching into virtual reality and artificial intelligence.

Jeff Bezos


The founder of Amazon is a trailblazer in the realm of e-commerce and technology. He transformed an online bookstore into a global retail giant. Bezos' strategic vision extends beyond retail, encompassing ventures like cloud computing with Amazon Web Services. If his events today are as good as Amazon Prime Delivery, everyone's in for a treat.

Adrian Newey


Adrian Newey, a distinguished figure in Formula 1 engineering, is renowned for his expertise in aerodynamics and car design. With an illustrious career spanning multiple decades with F1 teams: Williams, McLaren and Red Bull Racing. He has helped win a whopping 12 World Constructor's Championships and 13 World Driver's championships.

Richard Branson


The charismatic entrepreneur and founder of the Virgin Group has made an unforgettable mark on the business world. Renowned for his adventurous spirit and thinking, Branson has successfully ventured into diverse industries, from music and airlines to space travel. This has given him strong insight into many different industries and we will love to hear about his expertise at TechCon.

Lisa Su


Dr. Lisa Su, the accomplished CEO of Advanced Micro Devices (AMD), is a leader in the semiconductor industry. Under her leadership, AMD has experienced remarkable growth. With a focus on innovation and competitiveness, Su's strategic vision has positioned the company as a key player in the development of high-performance computing technologies.

Tim Cook


Tim Cook, the CEO of Apple, exemplifies steady leadership in the tech industry. Taking the lead after Steve Jobs, Cook has guided Apple through a period of continued innovation. His strategic vision has helped the country branch out to different areas of consumer teachnology such as wearables, wireless earphones and even VR goggles.

Bobby Kotick


The CEO of Activision Blizzard has played a huge role in shaping the landscape of the gaming industry. Under his leadership, Activision has achieved remarkable success, becoming a leader in the world of gaming entertainment. Known for his strategic vision, Kotick has overseen the development and success of famous franchises such as Call of Duty.
